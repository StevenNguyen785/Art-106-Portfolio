Human Machine Collaberation

Title: Example of Mutualism Symbiosis Relationship

Medium: Adobe Photoshop

Future Short StoryThe story takes place inside a Digital Media company building which consists of digital artists which are humans working alongside semi-sentient robotic machines in 2052. The robotic machines are called Johnnys which were named after the company’s founder. The Johnnys can only refuel its mechanical components on its own when it is at half tank because it becomes malfunctional when it passes the half tank mark. It cannot replace its battery component on its own at all. Because once its battery in its compartment has been fully removed, then the semi-sentient is completely shut down. Therefore, it needs someone to replace its battery periodically when it needs to. Johnny #5 is the most agile out of all the Johnnys within the building for some unspecified reason. The programmers which designed the Johnny suggest that its unique human-like semi-sentient personality, compared to the other Johnnys has attracted a lot of human employees. Which makes it collaborate very well with others and becomes more agile to certain activities. This type of relationship is considered a Mutualism relationship because it benefits both collaborative partners where the machine needs partial help with it fueling and needs complete help replacing its battery from the human counterpart. Also, the human treats the machine more of a living thing then an object which gives the machine more of a personality and the ability to become more agile which benefits the machine. Vice versa, the machine helps the humans in return by becoming a security guard and tour guide for the human workers and visitors who are new to the building.
